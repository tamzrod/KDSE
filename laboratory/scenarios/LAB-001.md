## Purpose

This scenario validates that KDSE:

1. **Follows its methodology** - Foundation first, not implementation
2. **Distinguishes knowledge types** - Facts vs Assumptions vs Gaps
3. **Assigns evidence strength** - Not everything is equally certain
4. **Stops at the right point** - No premature implementation
